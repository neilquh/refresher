Lineups for 2023-2024

The Warriors start the 2022 season with the same starting lineup they ended last season with in Steph Curry, Draymond Green, Kevon Looney, Andrew Wiggins, and Klay Thompson.Key rotational pieces on this roster include 3rd year SG Jordan Poole and seasoned, 4-time champion Andre Igoudala. Perhaps most notable about the Warriors roster, though, is the number of young, high upside players they have. Namely, Jonathan Kuminga, Moses Moody and Patrick Baldwin Jr, all of whom are under age 21.
